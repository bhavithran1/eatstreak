The retention problem

Restaurants spend a fortune winning a guest —
then lose them after one meal.

$0

Most diners never return after their first visit. Acquisition cost is paid once and almost never recovered.

5–7×

It costs five to seven times more to win a new customer than to keep an existing one coming back.

Punch cards get lost. Loyalty apps go unused. Restaurants have no real-time picture of who their regulars are.

How Eatstreak works

Three taps to a habit.

No app download required to start. No clunky sign-ups. The streak does the work.
